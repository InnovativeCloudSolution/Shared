BrightGauge Platform Migration - Important Update
Important: BrightGauge Dashboard Migration
Your dashboard is moving to a new platform
We are currently migrating our BrightGauge instance from the US platform to the AU platform to provide you with better performance and service.

What's changing:

Old URL: https://manganoit.brightgauge.co/

New URL: https://manganoit.brightgauge.au/

Good news: Your dashboards will remain exactly the same - only the web address is changing!

Action Required:

You will receive an email from donotreply@brightgauge.com with your new dashboard link. Simply click the link in that email and bookmark the new dashboard location.

No Re-enrollment Required:

Since you already have BrightGauge login accounts, there's no need to re-enroll. Your existing account will automatically carry across to the new platform.

You can access your new dashboard immediately at: https://manganoit.brightgauge.au/

Important Timeline:

The old platform will be turned off on November 30th, 2024. Please update your bookmarks before this date.
